# Capstone 2025 Report Structure

Follow these section-specific requirements:

### 1. Introduction & Stakeholder Context (3-5 pages)
- Background & Motivation: Why it matters in the real world.
- Problem Statement: Complexity of the addressed problem.
- Objectives: Final goals.
- Scope & Limitations: Boundaries.
- Stakeholders & Expected Benefits.

### 2. Related Theory and Research (3-5 pages)
- Review of existing knowledge/prior work.
- Comparison with existing systems (strengths/weaknesses).
- Gap Linkage: Research/technology gaps filled by this project.

### 3. System Design (5-8 pages)
- System Architecture: Block diagrams, workflows, UML.
- Components: Hardware, software, databases, networking.
- Design Considerations: Scalability, usability, sustainability.

### 4. Implementation (5-8 pages)
- Development process: Modules and algorithms built.
- Tools & Frameworks: Languages, libraries, platforms.
- Challenges & Resolutions: Technical difficulties overcome.
- Evidence: Screenshots, logs, demo references.

### 5. Testing and Experimentation (3-5 pages)
- Plan, Setup, Metrics, and specific Test Cases.

### 6. Results and Analysis (3-5 pages)
- Presentation: Tables, charts, graphs.
- Interpretation: Patterns and significance.
- Limitations & Lessons learned.

### 7. Project Management and Teamwork (1 page)
- Timeline, Work division, Communication, and Reflection.

### 8. Ethics, Privacy, and Professional Considerations (1 page)
- Ethical issues, Standards/Laws, Environmental impact, and IP.

### 9. Risk and Resource Management (1 page)
- Risks, Adaptations, Resources, and Cost Considerations.

### 10. Conclusion and Future Work (1-2 pages)
- Achievements, Objectives met, Applications, and Future directions.
