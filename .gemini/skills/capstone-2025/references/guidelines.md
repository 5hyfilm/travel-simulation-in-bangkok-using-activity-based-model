# Capstone 2025 General Guidelines

## 1. Language and Tone
- **Formal Academic English**: Clear, concise, and grammatically correct.
- **Style**: Avoid casual language, bullet-only descriptions, or incomplete sentences. Use consistent tense throughout.

## 2. Figures and Diagrams
- **Accuracy**: Must be directly related to the content.
- **Formatting**: Each figure must have a Number, Title, and Caption (e.g., *Figure 1: System Architecture*).
- **Referencing**: You MUST refer to all figures in the main text (e.g., "...as shown in Figure 1").

## 3. References and Citations
- **Style**: Use IEEE or APA style consistently.
- **Integrity**: Every in-text citation must have a corresponding entry in the References section.
- **Academic Honesty**: Cite all sources properly (figures, data, text). Direct quotations must be in quotation marks.

## 4. Formatting
- **Structure**: Sections must follow the specific order defined in the template.
- **Table of Contents**: Must be included with correct page numbers.
- **Pagination**: Continuous throughout the document.
